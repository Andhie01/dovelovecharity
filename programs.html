Feeding Program

We serve 2 meals a day, and by God's provision, sometimes even 3. On difficult days, we at least ensure one nourishing meal to keep our children going. We provide our elders with monthly food packages to sustain them.

Education Support

With 3 dedicated teachers, we run nursery to Primary 5 classes in our temporary structures, giving every child a chance to learn and dream big. And we pay school fees for some children studying from different schools.

Faith & Mentorship

We guide our children spiritually and emotionally, helping them find strength in prayer and grow with faith, hope, and values that last a lifetime.

Emergency Care

For children facing abuse or abandonment, we offer shelter, clothing, and safety while surrounding them with warmth, prayers, and love.

Impact So Far

By God’s grace and through kind hearts like yours, we’ve provided food, learning, and safety for 50 children and counting. School fees, learning materials, clothes and shoes for over 200 children and counting. Food and care for 30 elderly people and counting. Together, we are rewriting their story.

Projects in the Pipeline

1. Clean Water Project - Every Drop Counts

Goal: Provide a sustainable source of clean, safe drinking water for Dove Love and the surrounding community.

Why It Matters: Borehole access in our community is extremely poor. Since the area lies near lakes, authorities assume water is readily available-yet this water is contaminated and unsafe. Children and families are left with no choice but to use this dirty water, leading to frequent outbreaks of waterborne diseases. The health and lives of our children are constantly at risk.

How You Can Help: With your support, we can drill a reliable borehole or install a community water purification system, ensuring access to clean water that will safeguard the well-being of our children and uplift the entire community.

2. Solar Power Project - Light Up Their World

Goal: Install a complete solar energy system at Dove Love Orphanage.

Why It Matters: Our community has no access to electricity. Once the sun sets, we are plunged into total darkness every night. This not only hinders evening activities like studying and preparing meals, but also exposes our children to insecurity and fear. With a reliable solar system, we can power essential lighting, charge devices, and create a safer, more hopeful environment.

How You Can Help: Your support can bring lasting light to our orphanage-fueling hope, education, and security through clean, sustainable energy.

3. Permanent Shelter & Learning Structures - Build Their Future

Goal: Construct stable housing and classrooms for the children.

Why It Matters: Current structures are made of iron sheets and wooden poles-hot,some are grass hutched, unsafe, and easily damaged. Permanent buildings will provide safety, comfort, and a better learning environment for our children.

4.Food Security Through Agriculture - Grow to Nourish

Goal: Acquire land and tools to start farming, growing our own food, instead of buying everyday.

Why It Matters: We rely on unpredictable food donations. With our own farm, we can grow nutritious food year-round, teach children sustainable farming skills, and become more self-reliant.
