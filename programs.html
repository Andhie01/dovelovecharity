What We Do

Our Programs

Transforming lives through love, education, nutrition, and faith-driven community care in Uganda, East Africa.

Active Programs

Caring for the Whole Child

From daily meals to spiritual mentorship every program nurtures body, mind, and spirit.

🍽

Feeding Program

We serve 2 meals a day and by God's provision, sometimes 3. We also provide monthly food packages to elderly members of our community.

📚

Education Support

With 3 dedicated teachers, we run nursery to Primary 5 classes. We also pay school fees for children attending other schools.

🙏

Faith & Mentorship

Growing minds, hearts, and futures rooted in faith. We guide our children spiritually and emotionally every step of the way.

🏠

Emergency Care

For children facing abuse or abandonment we offer immediate shelter, clothing, and safety surrounded with warmth and prayer.

🏠50+Children in our care
📚200+Children supported with school fees
❤30+Elderly people served
🌍300+Lives touched since founding

By God's grace and through kind hearts like yours, we are rewriting their story.

Projects in the Pipeline

Each project addresses an urgent need and your support makes it possible.

01

Clean Water

The ProblemBorehole access is almost non-existent. Lake water is contaminated, causing frequent waterborne illness among our children.

Your ImpactFund a borehole or purification system to protect the health of our children and community.

02

Solar Power

The ProblemOur community has zero electricity. Every night the children are plunged into darkness no light to study by.

Your ImpactFund a complete solar energy system bringing light, security, and opportunity to our orphanage.

03

Permanent Shelter & Classrooms

The ProblemCurrent structures are iron sheets on wooden poles hot, unsafe, and easily damaged. Some children sleep in grass-thatched huts.

Your ImpactContribute to permanent buildings that provide safety, comfort, and proper learning spaces.

04

Food Security Through Agriculture

The ProblemWe rely on unpredictable donations for food. There is no certainty month to month.

Your ImpactHelp us acquire land and tools to farm our own food and build lasting self-reliance.
