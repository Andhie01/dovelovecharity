Where It Began

Our Full Story

Dove Love Charity Organization began as a vision sparked by the pain and hardship of Andrew Odoi. Growing up in poverty on Buvuma Island, he knows what it means to be forgotten. Raised by a single mother after his father's disappearance, life revolved around survival.

Every day, he walked over 10 kilometres to reach the nearest government primary school surviving on a single meal a day, or none at all. By God's grace, he made it through high school on bursaries and attended university through a government loan scheme.

...

As time passed, the government imposed restrictions on fishing crippling the once-thriving island economy. Families who had relied on fishing for generations found themselves destitute, unable to provide for their children.

Tragically, a high spread of HIV added to the crisis, leaving children orphaned and alone. Some parents, overwhelmed by financial ruin, were forced to abandon their children. Witnessing this suffering stirred something deep within Andrew.

Alongside a colleague, he embarked on a mission to make a difference. Together, they started Dove Love Charity Organizationsecuring land and erecting structures to provide shelter, education, and nourishment to the children of Uganda.

Leadership

Meet Andrew Odoi

Andrew Odoi

Co-Founder, Dove Love Charity Organization

Andrew Odoi is a Ugandan teacher and Child Defender. His personal experience of poverty and hardship on Buvuma Island fuels his unwavering mission to serve children who are orphaned or neglected. With faith and fierce compassion, he is creating safe spaces and educational paths for Uganda's most vulnerable children because he was once one of them.
