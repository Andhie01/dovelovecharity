Welcome to Dove Love Charity Organization

Bringing hope, love, and a future to orphaned children and vulnerable communities in Uganda.

Who we are

Dove Love Charity Organization is a grassroots non profit based in Uganda, committed to uplifting orphaned and vulnerable children. We believe every child deserves love, education, and the chance to build a brighter future. Initiated by Andrew alongside a colleague, out of a personal journey of pain and hope, we operate with deep community roots and a clear mission to care, educate, and empower.

What we do

We care for over 50 children at our sanctuary, and the number continues to grow. We provide shelter, basic education, and emotional support. Additionally, we've reached and helped over 200 other children by providing school fees, clothes, shoes, and school materials like books and pencils. We've also supported over 30 elderly people with food and care. Our mission is holistic we nurture, teach, and uplift.

Our impact

Since our founding, Dove Love has brought light to over 300 lives. We’ve enrolled dozens of children in school, provided thousands of meals, and created a space of love and belonging. Every action we take is made possible by compassionate hearts like yours.

Be part

Become part of the Dove Love family. You can support by donating, sponsoring a child, volunteering, or partnering with us to reach more communities. No act is too small to make a difference.
