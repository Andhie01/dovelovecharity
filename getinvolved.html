Daily Bread & Emergencies

Your support helps us provide food, learning materials, and urgent healthcare for the 50 children under our care.

  • $330 covers a month’s supply of rice for the 50 children
  • $100 provides beans for the entire month for the 50 children
  • $30 secures sugar for the whole month for the 50 children
  • $40 ensures porridge flour for daily breakfast for the whole month for 50 children

$500 can fully sponsor nutritious meals for all 50 children for an entire month.

Every gift brings immediate relief—and lasting hope. Thank you for standing with us.
